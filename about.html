About Us

Learn more about our mission, vision, and the team behind Helping Hand

Who We Are

Helping Hand is a dedicated organization committed to recognizing and addressing the signs of child sexual assault. Our team comprises passionate individuals from various backgrounds, all unified by the goal of protecting and supporting vulnerable children.

Our Story

Founded in 2024, Helping Hand was established by a group of concerned citizens who recognized the urgent need for better awareness and resources for child sexual assault. Since our inception, we have worked tirelessly to educate communities and provide support to those in need.

Our journey began with a simple mission: to create a safer world for children. Over the years, we have expanded our reach, developed comprehensive programs, and built a strong network of volunteers and partners dedicated to this cause.

Our Mission

Our mission is to empower individuals and communities to recognize and respond to the signs of child sexual assault. We aim to provide the necessary resources, education, and support to ensure that every child can live free from fear and abuse.

We believe that through awareness, education, and collective action, we can make a significant difference in the lives of children affected by sexual assault. Our programs are designed to equip caregivers, educators, and community members with the tools they need to identify and address abuse effectively.
