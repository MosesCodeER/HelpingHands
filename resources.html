Resources

Find valuable resources and support for child sexual assault victims

Hotlines

Here is a list of hotlines you can contact for immediate assistance:

  • National Child Abuse Hotline: 1-800-4-A-CHILD (1-800-422-4453)
  • Childhelp National Child Abuse Hotline: 1-888-222-7273
  • National Sexual Assault Hotline: 1-800-656-HOPE (1-800-792-7324)

Support Groups

Support groups provide a safe space for victims and their families to share their experiences and receive emotional support. Here are some organizations that offer support groups:

  • RAINN (Rape, Abuse & Incest National Network)
  • Childhelp
  • 1in6 (Support for Male Survivors of Sexual Assault)

Online Resources

Online platforms can provide a wealth of information and support. Here are some valuable online resources:

  • Child Welfare Information Gateway
  • Stop It Now!
  • Darkness to Light
